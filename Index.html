Sphagetti Bolognese

A classic italian dish that's hearty and flavourful

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 400g canned tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Boil the Spaghetti according to the package Instructions
  2. In a pan, saute onions and garlic until golden
  3. Add minced beef and cook until browned
  4. Pour in the canned tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve over Spaghetti
